HamBurger

Description

This is the best HamBurger recipe currently found at the moment

Ingredients

  • Meat Patty
  • Cheese
  • Buns
  • Tomateo

Steps

  1. Get the buns ready
  2. Put meat patty on bottom bun
  3. Add cheese atop
  4. Add Tomateo atop again
  5. Add top bun atop of previous Ingredients
